I often think devotion lives in the completed bow — the moment someone has fully lowered themselves before what they believe in. This film asks whether the more honest part is actually the descent itself, the reaching down before it's finished.

Nine seconds. Hanuman kneels before Shiva as Nataraja, a stotram playing underneath, the gesture held right at the midpoint and never let all the way down. Respect, caught still falling.
