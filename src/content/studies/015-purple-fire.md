Do you need a face to feel like you've met someone? This is the shortest piece in the whole set, and maybe the clearest test of that question.

Six seconds. A pure silhouette wrapped in shifting purple and pink flame, no features, no name, nothing to call it by. And somehow it still feels like a presence in the room, not an absence.
