## Core Question

At what exact frame does weather stop being weather and start being a weapon?

## Concept

Eighteen seconds. A hand closes around a live lightning bolt, and the clip ends just as that bolt starts forging itself into a glowing sword — caught choosing its future, not yet living in it. When the frame cuts, it's still lightning. It's just lightning with plans.

## Why the Sword Never Shows Up

A finished glowing sword is a prop shelf — every fantasy short has one sitting on it. A bolt of electricity mid-decision about becoming one is a process, and processes are the only thing this reel is actually interested in collecting. Cut before the forge finishes and the piece stays about becoming instead of about the merchandise.

## Connection to the Cuts

The elemental mirror of A Merge in Progress: same move (a thing caught mid-transformation), opposite dialect — sacred stillness swapped for kinetic force. Together they're the clearest proof the reel's bit survives the jump from temple to thunderstorm.
